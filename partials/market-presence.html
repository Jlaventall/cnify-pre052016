Online Presence

Not all brands need an office in China. Having a full fledged online presence can make an important difference though. Addressing your target consumer by being on the right platforms with the right information and available to interact is what CNify specializes in. With in-market/in-language knowledgeable staff, we represent brands, destinations, businesses and organizations as their Chinese presence online. We handle all aspects of online presence including setting and managing accounts, creating, translating and posting content and engaging with your target audience online.

Fulfillment

For brands interested in selling directly to China, CNify offers fulfillment and export on demand services. Instead of taking a huge capital investment and associated risk of selling large quantities, CNify can fulfill consumer demand at the individual and group buying level. Group buying is a recent phenomena online, and groups of Chinese consumers specifically search out foreign sourced products. Your brand can become part of these group buying networks and CNify offers the necessary fulfillment infrastructure to ensure your products arrive safely to consumers in China.
